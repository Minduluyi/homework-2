Work

My recent work is developing a website and mobile application for Indigenous Communications Group.

Project one

www.Yulngu.com website. Yulngu is a website designed to meet the needs of clients who have Aboriginal Art to resell. These clients have collected Aboriginal Art pieces over the years. We have set up a collation program where clients can register their art for sale. They must first become members on the Yulngu Fine Art Gallery website which enable them to arrange a 30,60 or 90 day agency agreement for us to sell their art pieces online.

Project two

app.yulngu.com (Mobile App) The yulngu mobile app is a facility to help Aboriginal Artists upload and catalogue their art for sale. It enables artists to sell their art direct ro customers, without leaving country. The application has functions to conducts sales of art locally. This means Artists will be able to conducts live exhibtions for local customers or we say "walk in" customers who compete with online customer.
