Project Two

Yulngu Fine Art Gallery

"THE LAND. THE ART. THE EXPERIENCE."

"EVERYDAY"

Yulngu Fine Art Gallery is a website designed to meet the needs of Artists and clients where the gallery acts as broker to represent local artists and those clients who have art for sale. These clients have collected Aboriginal Art pieces over the years. We have set up a collation program where clients can register their art for sale. They must first become members on the Yulngu Fine Art Gallery website which enable them to arrange a 30,60 or 90 day agency agreement for us to sell their art pieces online.

Project Three

app.yulngu.com (Mobile App) The yulngu mobile app is a facility to help Aboriginal Artists upload and catalogue their art for sale. It enables artists to sell their art direct ro customers, without leaving country. The application has functions to conducts sales of art locally.
